Overview

The mountain wind is a simple and at the same time five-star hotel in Central Asia, located in an ideal location on the corner of Kabanbay Batyr and Seifullin streets in the very center of green Almaty. An impressive city, although no longer the capital of Kazakhstan, it is clear that this is the center of the country's culture and heritage. Museums, parks, cafes and restaurants abound. The city is a paradise for those looking for a cultural getaway amid stunning scenery and fabulous architecture. The mountain wind hotel is a unique combination of cozy rooms with good service and traditional hospitality.
